WEEKS 1-3: Foundation. Build Your Base

Time: 15-20 min/session
3-4 session/week

What You'll Feel:

Less stiffness after long day

Standing up feels lighter

Learn proper breathing & positioning

Focus Areas:

Hip flexor release

Basic external rotation

Gentle adductor opening

Psoas decompression

WEEKS 4-7: Progression. Active ranges

Time: 15-20 min/session
3-4 session/week

What You'll Feel:

Squats getting noticeably deeper

Can sit cross-legged without pain

Hip circles feel smooth and easy

Focus Areas:

Internal rotation work

Deep hip openers

Movement control in new range

WEEKS 8-10: Mobilize & Advance

Sessions: 20-25 min,
3-4x/week

What You'll Feel: 

Movements feel natural again

Lower back pain significantly reduced

Increased range of motion

Focus Areas:

Resch Split prep & progressions

Hamstring & adductor focus

Full hip opening sequences

WEEKS 11-13 Mastery. Advanced — Upgrade Your Range

Sessions: 20-25 min,
3-4x/week

What You'll Feel: 

Move with power AND flexibility

Progress in amplitude & control

Complete hip freedom in all directions

Focus Areas:

Splits progressions

Advanced active flexibility

Strength in end ranges

Full movement integration

@use "sass:math";

@mixin resize(
  $min,
  $max,
  $property: font-size,
  $min-vw: 340px,
  $max-vw: 1400px
) {
  $min-no-unit: math.div($min, ($min * 0 + 1));
  $max-no-unit: math.div($max, ($max * 0 + 1));
  $min-vw-no-unit: math.div($min-vw, ($min-vw * 0 + 1));
  $max-vw-no-unit: math.div($max-vw, ($max-vw * 0 + 1));

  $min-rem: math.div($min-no-unit, 16);
  $max-rem: math.div($max-no-unit, 16);

  $slope: math.div(
    $max-rem - $min-rem,
    math.div($max-vw-no-unit - $min-vw-no-unit, 16)
  );
  $y-axis: -1 * $min-vw-no-unit * math.div($slope, 16) + $min-rem;

  #{$property}: clamp(
    #{$min-rem}rem,
    #{$y-axis}rem + #{$slope * 100}vw,
    #{$max-rem}rem
  );
}
