I can barely touch my toes. Is this course too advanced for me?

+

Not at all.
About 80% of our students start exactly where you are — stiff, tight, and unsure where to begin. The program is built to:
• adapt to your current level
• progress gradually and safely
• help you feel stronger and more mobile step by step
You don’t need to be flexible to start — flexibility comes from the process.

How can I watch the workouts on my TV?

+

You can easily stream the workouts using one of these options:
1. Open the platform directly through your Smart TV browser
2. Use your phone or laptop + Smart TV with Cast or Screen Mirroring
3. Connect your device to the TV using an HDMI cable
You can also train from a PC or laptop — just open the platform link sent to your email.
